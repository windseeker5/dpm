Event Notifications Visual Test

Interactive demonstration of real-time payment and signup notifications

Test Features

  • Payment Notifications: Green gradient with payment confirmation
  • Signup Notifications: Blue gradient with registration details
  • Auto-dismiss: Notifications disappear after 10 seconds
  • Manual dismiss: Click the X button to close immediately
  • Hover pause: Hover to pause auto-dismiss timer
  • Mobile responsive: Full-width notifications on mobile devices
  • Stacking limit: Maximum 5 notifications shown at once
  • Smooth animations: Slide-in from right with scale effect
