Usage Guidelines

When to Use Alerts
  • Success: Form submissions, successful operations
  • Info: General information, tips
  • Warning: Caution messages, validation warnings
  • Danger: Errors, critical issues
Best Practices
  • Use clear, actionable language
  • Include relevant icons for better recognition
  • Make dismissible when appropriate
  • Don't overuse - limit to important messages
